Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice (or kimchi-bokeumbab) is a delicious and versatile Korean fried rice dish that's sure to become a favorite. You can use ham, bacon, or spam instead of ground beef, add more veggies, or spice things up with Korean gochujang paste!

Ingredients

  • Oils
  • Beef
  • Green Onion
  • Kimchi
  • Gochujang
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook beef and white parts of green onion in oil
  2. Add kimchi and gochujang, cook until soft and stir with rice
  3. Cook until rice is now heated through and finish with seasame oil and green onions
  4. Cook egg and serve over the fried rice
